Instructions

The visualization shows information about 31 exoplanets

Three types of exoplanets are shown ( Super Earth, Nepytune-like and Terrestrial). Each is shown with a different planet image

A legend is placed on the bottom left to show the different planet types used

Distance in light years of the exoplanet from Earth is shown in the axis

Radius of the planet shown depicts the size of the planet with respect to Earth's

Hovering over the planets will show more information about the respective planet (name, distance from earth, radius wrt Earth)

Clicking on a planet will populate the sections below -

Mass with respect to Earth - The planet's mass is shown against planet Earth on a weighing scale type visualization

Planet Information - The planet's information is shown in a table format on the bottom left section

The selected planet is shown with a surrounding ring

You can come back to this instructions anytime by clicking on Instructions on the top left corner of the screen

Click on the X icon or the button below to begin exploring Exoplanet exhibit !
